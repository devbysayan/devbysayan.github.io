10 — FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build a website? +
Depending on the project, a basic landing page takes 3–5 days, a business website 7–10 days, and a full web application 2–4 weeks. I always give you a clear timeline before starting.
Do you provide hosting and domain? +
I can help you set up hosting and domain. I recommend platforms like Hostinger, Cloudflare, or Netlify based on your needs. The cost of hosting/domain is separate from my development fee.
What is the payment structure? +
I require 20% advance payment before starting work. The remaining 80% is paid upon project delivery. I accept payment via UPI, bank transfer, or any preferred method.
Will my website be mobile friendly? +
Absolutely. Every website I build is fully responsive and tested across mobile, tablet, and desktop screens. Mobile-first design is standard in all my projects.
Do you offer revisions? +
Yes! Revisions are included in every plan — Starter gets 2, Professional gets 5, and Enterprise gets unlimited revisions. I work with you until you are 100% satisfied.
Can you redesign my existing website? +
Yes! Website redesign is one of my specialties. I'll analyze your current site, understand your goals, and deliver a modern, high-converting redesign that keeps your brand identity intact.
